About Ride or Die

Here at Ride or Die our aim is to help people who are a little older, or maybe haven't dated in awhile by bringing them together. Everyone deserves a to have someone to love and in a lot of causes people have lost loved ones. Our goal is to find these people someone new to love with similar interest to them so they can enoy the rest of their lives.

 

We were founded in 2020 and have set up successful relationships for many couples already all over Ireland.
